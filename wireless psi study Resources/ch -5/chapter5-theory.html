RADAR Systems
Radio Detection And Ranging – Deep Notes
📡 RADAR = Radio Detection And Ranging
  • Radio waves → Target → Echo → Detection
  • 2nd World War (1940s) માં વિકસાવ્યું
  • Frequency: 3 MHz – 300 GHz
  • સિદ્ધાંત: Echo (પ્રતિધ્વનિ) Reflection
⏱️ Pulse Parameters
  • Pulse Width (τ) – 0.1–10 μs
  • PRT – Pulse Repetition Time
  • PRF = 1/PRT (100–10000 Hz)
  • Duty Cycle = τ × PRF
📻 Doppler Effect
  • Target નજીક → Frequency વધે
  • Target દૂર → Frequency ઘટે
  • fd = 2Vr / λ
  • MTI – Moving Target Indication

📐 RADAR Range Equation

Most Important Formula Rmax = [Pt × G² × λ² × σ / (4π)³ × Smin]^(1/4)

R = c × t / 2 (Basic Range)
fd = 2Vr / λ (Doppler Frequency)
ΔR = c × τ / 2 (Range Resolution)
Runamb = c / (2 × PRF) (Max Unambiguous Range)

Satellite Communication
ઉપગ્રહ સંચાર સિસ્ટમ – Deep + Revision
🌍 Kepler's Laws
  • 1st: Elliptical orbit – Earth at focus
  • 2nd: Equal areas in equal times
  • 3rd: T² ∝ a³ (Harmonic Law)
  • T² = (4π²/GM) × a³
📡 GEO Satellite
  • ઊંચાઈ: 35,786 km
  • Period: 24 hours
  • 3 satellites = Global coverage
  • Delay ≈ 270 ms one-way
🔗 Uplink / Downlink
  • Uplink: Earth → Satellite
  • Downlink: Satellite → Earth
  • Transponder: Freq shift + Amplify
  • EIRP = Pt × Gt

Fibre Optics Engineering
ફાઇબર ઓપ્ટિક્સ – Principles to Measurements
🔷 TIR – Total Internal Reflection
  • n₁ > n₂ (Dense → Rare)
  • θ > θc → TIR (Light stays in fibre)
  • sinθc = n₂/n₁
  • Fibre optics નો મૂળ સિદ્ધાંત!
📏 Numerical Aperture
  • NA = √(n₁² – n₂²)
  • NA = n₀ sinθmax
  • SMF: NA = 0.1–0.2
  • MMF: NA = 0.2–0.5
🔧 Fibre Structure
  • Core – High n (Light travels)
  • Cladding – Low n (TIR for)
  • Buffer – Protection
  • Jacket – Outer protection

  • Persistence of Vision: 1/16 sec
  • PAL: 625 Lines, 25 fps, 50 Hz
  • NTSC: 525 Lines, 30 fps, 60 Hz

🎨 Color Formula
  • Y = 0.299R + 0.587G + 0.114B
  • R+G+B = White (Additive)
  • PAL SC: 4.43361875 MHz
  • NTSC SC: 3.58 MHz
📡 IF Frequencies (PAL India)
  • Video IF: 38.9 MHz
  • Audio IF: 33.4 MHz
  • Color SC IF: 34.47 MHz
  • Audio carrier: fv + 5.5 MHz

📐 Key Formulas

Microwave Engineering Formulas Z₀ = √(L/C) (Characteristic Impedance)
Γ = (ZL–Z₀)/(ZL+Z₀) (Reflection Coefficient)
VSWR = (1+|Γ|)/(1–|Γ|)
fc = c/2a (TE₁₀ cut-off)
λg = λ/√[1–(fc/f)²] (Guide wavelength)
vp × vg = c² (Phase × Group velocity)
Q = f₀/Δf (Cavity Q factor)

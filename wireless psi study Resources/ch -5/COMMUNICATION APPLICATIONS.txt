COMMUNICATION APPLICATIONS

Introduction

આ વિષય મુખ્યત્વે પાંચ મોટા ભાગમાં વહેંચાયેલો છે:

1. Television Engineering
   • Basics:

* Scanning (Interlaced Scanning)
* Composite Video Signal

• Camera Tubes:

* Vidicon
* Plumbicon
* (હાલમાં CCD Camera Sensors વધુ વપરાય છે, પરંતુ જૂના પ્રશ્નોમાં Vidicon અને Plumbicon પૂછાય છે.)

• Color Television:

* Three Primary Colors (Red, Green, Blue – RGB)
* Color Mixing
* PAL Standard
* NTSC Standard
* PAL અને NTSC વચ્ચેનો તફાવત

• Display Devices:

* CRT (Cathode Ray Tube)
* LCD (Liquid Crystal Display)
* LED (Light Emitting Diode)

---

2. Radar and Navigational Aids
   • Concept:

* RADAR (Radio Detection And Ranging)

• Types of Radar:

* Pulsed Radar
* CW (Continuous Wave) Radar
* MTI (Moving Target Indicator) Radar

• Doppler Effect:

* રાડાર દ્વારા વસ્તુની ગતિ (Speed) કેવી રીતે માપવામાં આવે છે.

• Radar Range Equation:

* રાડારની મહત્તમ રેન્જ શોધવાનું સૂત્ર.

---

3. Fiber Optic Communication
   • Principle:

* Total Internal Reflection (TIR)

• Components:

* Types of Fiber:

  * Single Mode Fiber
  * Multi Mode Fiber

* Light Sources:

  * LED
  * LASER

* Detectors:

  * PIN Photo Diode
  * Avalanche Photo Diode (APD)

• Losses in Optical Fiber:

* Attenuation
* Dispersion

---

4. Satellite Communication
   • Satellite Orbits:

* GEO (Geostationary Earth Orbit)
* MEO (Medium Earth Orbit)
* LEO (Low Earth Orbit)

• Communication System:

* Uplink
* Downlink
* Transponder

• Applications:

* GPS
* DTH Television
* VSAT

---

5. Mobile / Cellular Communication
   • Cellular Concepts:

* Frequency Reuse
* Handoff (Handover)
* Cell Splitting

• GSM Architecture:

* SIM (Subscriber Identity Module)
* HLR (Home Location Register)
* VLR (Visitor Location Register)
* BTS (Base Transceiver Station)
* BSC (Base Station Controller)
* MSC (Mobile Switching Centre)

• Mobile Communication Generations:

* 1G
* 2G
* 3G
* 4G
* 5G

Summary:
Communication Applications subject mainly covers Television Engineering, Radar Systems, Fiber Optic Communication, Satellite Communication, and Mobile/Cellular Communication along with their principles, components, standards, applications, and modern communication technologies.

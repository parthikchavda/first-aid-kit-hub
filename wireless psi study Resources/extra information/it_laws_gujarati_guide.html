માહિતી ટેકનોલોજી અધિનિયમ, 2000 (IT Act 2000)

Information Technology Act, 2000 – ભારતનો પ્રથમ સાઈબર કાયદો

▼
📌 પ્રાથમિક માહિતી

IT Act 2000 ભારત સંસદ દ્વારા 9 મે 2000 ના રોજ પ્રમુખ K.R. Narayanan ના હસ્તાક્ષર સાથે અમલમાં આવ્યો. 17 ઓક્ટોબર 2000 ના રોજ સૂચિત. ભારત IT ક્ષેત્રમાં વિશ્વનો 12મો દેશ છે જે પૃથક સાઈબર કાયદો ધરાવે છે. મૂળ Act માં 94 Sections, 13 Chapters, 4 Schedules હતાં.

ઉદ્દેશ્ય
  • ઈ-ગવર્નન્સ, ઈ-કોમર્સ, ઈ-બેન્કિંગ ને કાનૂની આધાર આપવો
  • ડિજિટલ સહી (Digital Signature) ને કાનૂની માન્યતા
  • ઈ-રેકર્ડ ને કાગળ-આધારિત દસ્તાવેજ સમકક્ષ ગણવો
  • સાઈબર ગુનાઓ નિર્ધારિત કરી સજા નક્કી કરવી
  • Certifying Authorities (CA) ની સ્થાપના
  • Cyber Appellate Tribunal ની સ્થાપના

Promotion and Regulation of Online Gaming Act, 2025

▼
📌 ઐતિહાસિક પૃષ્ઠભૂ

ભારત – 591 Million gamers (વૈશ્વિક ગેમર્સ ના 20%). 2023 – ₹2.2 Billion market. 2028 – ₹8.6 Billion expected. 9.5 Billion app downloads 2023. FIU-IND ₹4,000 crore suspicious gaming transactions 2023 traced. ₹20,000 crore/year user losses. 2025 – 45 crore લોકો negatively affected.

ગેમ્સ ના પ્રકાર

🏆 E-Sports

Organized tournaments, Skill-based competitive digital games. Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports – guidelines, training academies

🎲 Online Social Games

Skill-based entertainment/social games. Wordle, Puzzle games. Union Gov – promote safe, age-appropriate platforms

💰 Online Money Games

Financial stakes, Chance/Skill/Both. Dream11, Poker, Rummy. Players pay fees – expectation of monetary gain. BANNED under new Act

🚫 Real Money Gaming (RMG)

Comprehensive Ban on all RMG – games of skill, chance, or mixed nature. Eliminates earlier "skill vs chance" loopholes

Exam-Oriented Key Points – IT & Cyber Field

Most frequently asked in GPSC / UPSC

▼
1. Shreya Singhal v. Union of India (2015)

SC – Section 66A = Article 19(1)(a) violation. Struck down. Chilling effect on free speech. Jan Vishwas Act 2023 – formally removed.

2. Justice K.S. Puttaswamy v. UOI (2017)

9-Judge bench. Right to Privacy = Fundamental Right under Article 21. Basis of DPDP Act 2023. Informational Privacy, Decisional Privacy.

3. Anuradha Bhasin v. UOI (2020)

Internet = Article 19(1)(a) & 19(1)(g). Internet shutdown must be proportionate, time-bound, subject to judicial review. Review orders – periodically publish.

4. L. Chandra Kumar Case (1997)

High Courts & Supreme Court – Judicial Review = Basic Structure. Tribunals (IT Act Cyber Tribunal) cannot oust HC jurisdiction. APTEL appeals subject to HC/SC.

5. S.R. Bommai (1994) + Disaster Context

COVID-19 2020 – Disaster Management Act + National Disaster = Emergency like situation without invoking Article 352. Section 6 NDMA powers extensive.

6. Section 69A – App Ban

59 Chinese Apps (TikTok) June 2020, 43 Nov 2020, 54 including Free Fire Feb 2022 – National Security, Section 69A. 1,524 gaming/betting apps blocked by June 2025.
